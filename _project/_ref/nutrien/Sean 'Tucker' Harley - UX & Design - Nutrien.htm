Nutrien

Nutrien is the world's largest provider of crop inputs and services, with annual revenue topping $37 billion in 2022.

International Operating Standards
Nutrien has close to 1300 branch locations across North America, each with its own SharePoint site to store completed safety documents. The goal of this project was to simplify the process of downloading required global documents and uploading completed forms, checklists, and meeting summaries.
Documenting Knowledge
The Product Owner held detailed knowledge of the SharePoint structure and other systems in his head, requiring constant explanation. I documented his expertise to share with the team.
Design Exploration
The design brief (Jira Ticket) was very directive and after several attempts to use an existing modal interaction pattern, it was decided we would explore having the Operating Standards functionality as a separate page.
The New Section
I visually connected the global Operating Standards repository (left column) with the user's local branch files (right column). This removed the confusion experienced in the previous designs, because the person would need to navigate to new screens to accomplish the download/uploading tasks. 
Multi-file Uploader
When designing the uploader I made sure that the desktop and mobile versions of the component were the same size. This allowed for seamless transition between breakpoints and meant the development team could could focus on the functionality the component. 
Usability Testing
During usability testing, 5 of 6 users were alarmed by the "Overwrite" option for existing files, as part of the upload process, as it could lead to failed audits. This and other insights assisted in guiding the product roadmap for Phase 3.
